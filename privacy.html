When you visit this website, GitHub's servers automatically collect and store certain technical information in server log files. This may include:

This data is collected by GitHub, not by me, and is processed in accordance with GitHub's General Privacy Statement. GitHub may store this data on servers in the United States or other countries.

The legal basis for this processing is my legitimate interest in operating a publicly accessible website (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). I have no influence over GitHub's data collection and refer you to GitHub's privacy policy for further information.
